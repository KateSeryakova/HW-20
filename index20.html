Breakroom mobile app group project

Social Hangouts for Remote Workers

The problem

The Covid-19 pandemic forever changed the traditional, in-office work model. Remote and hybrid work has become the norm and is expected to become a permanent fixture in the modern workforce. We have observed that remote workers often feel lonely and isolated during their workday, which has a negative impact on their mental health. How might we improve remote work so that those who suffer from feelings of loneliness and isolation can find a way to create community and friendships?

The solution

We believe that creating a meet-up app for remote workers will help them feel less lonely during their workday and create opportunities to connect socially.

    User Persona

    ANNIE JACKSON 30 years young, remote worker Washington, DC

    Bio

    Annie is a single young professional, who recently graduated college and moved to Washington DC from her hometown of Atlanta. Eager to begin blooming in her new city, she has recently been sent home to work remotely due to the change in her company's downsize of office space. As a social butterfly, she thrives in a community of people and had a strong and active social life in Atlanta. While working remotely in DC, she has not had the chance to grow her social circle and is looking for a new group of friends to connect with.

    Goals

    Meet new people in her city and rebuild her social circle with genuine friendships

    Behavior

    She works solo due to her 100% remote job and feels isolated from her friends. Self-motivated and a social butterfly; she enjoys socializing with others, but due to her role as a remote worker, she doesn't get to meet many people outside of her work day. Likes to volunteer, dance, and do yoga Listens to self-improvement and true crime podcasts.

    Needs

    Meet new people after her recent relocation to Washington DC. Get out of the house to improve her mental health & stress. Wants to find a way to decompress with others after a long workday. Create genuine friendships by engaging with others.

    Frustrations

    Misses going out with her friends, wishes she can find a friend group. Tired of being home and feels isolated from her new city/community. Can't find a central place to meet other remote workers. Spends hours scrolling through social apps trying to find a local "could be friend".

  • Prototyping

    Primary features

    Create a profile

    Users create customized profiles that help them find matches based off factors such as location, reason for using the app, etc.

    Find and chat with a friend

    Users are able to swipe through profiles of other users in their area to add to their friend list & chat.

  • Testing

    Lo-fi testing

    We conducted 4 remote moderated user tests to understand how easy it is for the user to create a customized profile, add the friend and go to the chat screen. Our target users are remote working professionals ages 20-45.

    Tasks:

    Create a customized profile Add the co-worker to the contact list Go to the chat screen
